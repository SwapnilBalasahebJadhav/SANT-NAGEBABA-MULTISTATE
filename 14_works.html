GREEN INDIA
SHERE:

"Shri Sant Nagebaba Multistate Nature Initiative" In this country of ours, trees are being slaughtered, so it is having an adverse effect on nature. Of course, the rainfall has reduced. All our quality of life depends on the rain. Our Nagebaba Multistate is implementing an innovative initiative • In this deposit scheme, the account holder members who sincerely want to plant a tree, grow it, nurture it, grow nature, contribute to the national cause, have to deposit ten thousand rupees in Nagebaba Multistate. This deposit will be locked for five years. In exchange for your interest, the organization will plant a beautiful tree in your name or in the name of your relatives or in the memory of your ancestors, it will be nurtured for a total of five years and most importantly there will be a board of your name next to that tree. Also from time to time you will be called to the place where you are going to plant trees. Such a beautiful unforgettable | We are implementing the deposit scheme

ANNAPURNA DEPOSIT SCHEME
SHERE:

'Food donation is the best gift' Ladhu and sisters, if you look close to you, many men and women are deprived of food. The children of many do not take care of them, some of them have children outside, they are not able to cook. Nagebaba Multistate is participating in this work as a social responsibility as many people come to the city with patients to the hospital and do not see the whole scene of food being served in the city. We are implementing an innovative initiative for all of them. "Annapurna Deposit Scheme" You have to deposit twenty thousand rupees in Nagebaba Multistate for ten years on your birthday, on the occasion of your wedding anniversary, on the birthday of a relative, on the birthday of a relative, on the birthday of a friend's family, on the birthday of a leader or in the memory of your relatives who have passed away. But in return, find people who are very needy and give them food. It assures you that you will plan your home delivery properly. So let's also fix some amount of your earnings for food donation Your principal amount will be given back to you after the expiry of the deadline. As many members as possible should participate in performing virtuous deeds. 'Annadata Happy Bhava:'
